How this works:
• Credit Period = Time supplier gives you to pay (e.g. 30 days) → Due date auto-calculates from invoice date
• Advance Paid = Amount you paid before receiving goods (pre-payment to supplier)
• Payment Received = Amount received from party after invoice (if you're the seller) — leave 0 if not applicable
• Net Due = Total − Advance − Payment Received − TDS (auto)
